
# Sample Product Manual (v1.2)

## Account & Security
- Password Requirements: at least 12 characters, one number, one symbol.
- Two-Factor Authentication: Go to Settings > Security > 2FA to enable.

## Troubleshooting
- If you cannot receive verification emails, check spam folder or whitelist no-reply@example.com.
- For app crashes, collect logs from Help > Diagnostics and attach them in your support ticket.
